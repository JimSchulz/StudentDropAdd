Instructions for the Drop/Add application!

Here's what you can do with this application:
• Search the Class Schedule for courses.
• Add or Drop a course.
• Change the grade track for a course.

Dropping a Course

• Students are required to be registered in every block. Dropping a course must be replaced with another course.
• To drop a course, scroll to the “Course Drop and Grading Track Changes” section and use the drop-down list next to the course you wish to drop, and then choose “drop”.
• You will then be asked to add a new course. The “Course Drop and Grading Track Changes” section freezes until you choose and add a new course.
• Follow the instructions for adding a course through completion.
• Grading Track changes cannot be performed while executing a course drop. Any Grading Track changes performed while executing a course drop will be restored to their original values.
• You can always click “Cancel” to restore your initial course schedule.

Changing a Grading Track

• “Letter Grades” track applies to specific grades (like A, B-, C+, etc.) which impact your GPA.
• “Optional Grades” track applies to more general grades (like S/CR/NC) which may impact your GPA depending on grade received. Refer to the Grading Standards section of The Pathfinder.
• The “Audit” track does not give you a grade and does not impact your GPA.
• You can modify the grading track for a class by clicking on the corresponding drop-down list and choosing a different grading track, then clicking “Apply Drops and Grading Track Changes” at the bottom of this section.
• Confirm your change and press “OK” to process your change, or press “Cancel” to restore your initial grade track.

Adding a Course

• Go to the “Course Add” section. You should see your name and student ID number.
• If you happen to know the Term, CRN, and Grading Track of the class you wish to add, then just enter them here and click the Continue button.
• If you need to look-up the CRN (Course Reference Number) for the class you wish to add, press the “Search Class Schedule” button and then scroll down to the bottom of the page.
• Only the “Term” field is required, but please note that results are limited to the first 200 classes found, so include as many course identifiers as possible.
• Once you have entered your filter(s), press the “Class Search” button to load the results of the search.
• Find the class you wish to add and then click on the corresponding checkbox on the left.
• This will populate the chosen course’s CRN in the “Course Add” section above.
• From this point you can select your desired Grading Track and then press the “Continue” button.
• The course information for the Term and CRN is now displayed, so students can verify the selection before adding the class. Verify the class is correct and press the “Add Course” button. The class will be added to your schedule.
• Note that when starting the process of adding a class, the Term, CRN, and Grading Track fields become locked for the current transaction. To unlock them and start over, press the “Reset” button.

Consent

• Some classes require a consent before they can be dropped or added. Even a change to a Grading Track may require a consent.
• If a consent is required, an additional input field will appear in the appropriate section. Enter in the consent code and then proceed as normal.
• Depending on the class consent, you may need to contact your instructor or department chair, if unsure, please contact the Registrar’s Office for more information, registrar@coloradocollege.edu or 719-389-6610.
